Early Years Teacher

£25,000 p/a
ENQUIRE NOW
DESCRIPTION
The Early Years Department is a special place, where imagination comes alive, talents are nurtured and friendships are fostered. It is a happy vibrant place where your child’s days are ﬁlled with fun and laughter. The Curriculum Framework in the Early Years covers language and literacy skills, early number skills, personal and social development, physical development, creative development, and religious education. The Curriculum is based on the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) Framework recommended by the DfE. The children in the Early Years Department are also introduced to French and are encouraged to sing, speak and enjoy this new language. The department is taught by a qualified teacher with early years specialism, assisted by an Early Years practitioner.
