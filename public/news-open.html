In February, we had PSCHE week, where our children got involved in a range of fantastic activities. PSCHE stands for personal, social, citizenship and health education. It is an important and necessary part of our pupils’ education where we look forward to learning new skills and exploring topics outside of the regular curriculum. Let’s take a look at what our pupils got up to…
At the beginning of the week, the children had a great time learning about life skills. Prep 3 and 4 had a visit from the brilliant WoW Swimming Team as they joined together to learn the extremely important topic of water safety. Our pupils have lessons with the swim team weekly and love working with them. Educating them on this topic is paramount as summer fast approaches! Meanwhile, Prep 5 and 6 explored a foremost life skill, first aid and resuscitation. We believe first aid training in the early stages of education provides pupils with a vital knowledge base for dealing with any medical emergencies.
