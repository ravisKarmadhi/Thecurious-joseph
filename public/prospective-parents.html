Why choose us?

Prospective parents are always welcome to make an appointment to meet Mr Hood (Head Teacher) and view the School. We would be happy to answer any questions you may have and we fully appreciate that choosing a Nursery or School is a hugely important decision. Please visit our Contact Us page to arrange a visit or call us on 01782 417 533.
Entry to the Nursery, Pre-Preparatory and Preparatory Department is by interview. Children are invited to come and spend a day with us, during this time teaching staff have the opportunity to assess children in a relaxed way in order to gain a baseline to be used to plan for further development and progress.

JOE’S CLUB

Before and after school childcare facility

Joe’s Club is the Before and After School Childcare Facility for children at St Joseph’s Preparatory School and is provided for children whose parents need to drop them at School before 8.30am and collect them later than 3.25pm. It is open to all pupils at St Joseph’s Preparatory School and provision will be made for children with special needs.
