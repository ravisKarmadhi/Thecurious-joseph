NURSERY AND RECEPTION

Early Years

The leading Nursery in Stoke-On-Trent.

The Early Years are critical in a child’s development and so we provide a stimulating and high quality early years environment. Within a structured program of play and learning activities, the social, emotional, moral, physical and intellectual needs of every child are met. St Joseph’s Preparatory School Early Years Department contains two classes, Nursery and Reception. We are proud to be a leading nursery in Stoke-on-Trent.
